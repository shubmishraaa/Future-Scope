# 🔮 FutureScope
### *See your decisions before you make them*

Most people make life-changing decisions based on gut feeling, biased advice, or a flat pros/cons list. None of these show you what your life actually looks like 3 years down each path.

**FutureScope does.**

🌐 **Live Demo:** [v0-future-scope-web-app.vercel.app](https://v0-future-scope-web-app.vercel.app)

---

## 🤔 The Problem

When you face a major decision — drop out, switch careers, start a business, move cities — you're essentially choosing between futures you can't see. Traditional tools give you text. FutureScope gives you a visual simulation of your life.

---

## ✨ What It Does

You describe your situation, your decision, what matters most to you, and how bold you want to be. FutureScope generates **3 parallel futures** — Optimistic, Realistic, and Cautious — shown as branching timelines with month-by-month milestones.

- **Branching Timeline Visualization** — 3 paths fan out from "Today" like a river splitting into streams
- **AI-Powered Milestones** — Every milestone is generated by Gemini AI based on YOUR specific situation, not generic advice
- **Multi-Dimension Scoring** — Finance, Happiness, and Stress tracked across every milestone
- **Elaborate** — Click any milestone for a detailed AI paragraph about what your daily life looks, feels, and smells like at that point
- **Compare Mode** — Side-by-side comparison of all 3 paths at the final milestone
- **Dynamic Time Horizons** — Simulate 1 Year, 3 Years, or 5 Years into the future
- **Priority-Based** — Tell it what matters most (Money, Health, Career, Freedom) and the timelines reflect your values

## 🎯 How It Works

```
You describe your situation + decision
            ↓
Gemini AI generates 3 personalized timelines as JSON
            ↓
App renders branching SVG visualization
            ↓
Click any node to explore that milestone
            ↓
Hit Elaborate for a deep-dive AI narrative
            ↓
Compare all 3 paths side by side
```
